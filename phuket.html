Phuket

Click here to go Back!

Phuket City, on Phuket Island, is the capital of Thailand’s Phuket Province. In the Old Town, Thalang Road is lined with colorful 19th-century shophouses and Sino-Portuguese buildings. Built in 1903 by a wealthy tin merchant, Baan Chinpracha mansion has Italian floor tiles, shuttered windows and antique furniture. Set in a 1930s manor, the Thai Hua Museum has exhibits on Phuket’s culture and history.

Go Surfing in Phuket

Surf to your heart’s content in Surf House Phuket’s wave pool where you can ride waves continuously. Practice your surfing (or wipe out) skills and enjoy non-stop surf action and trendy music. They currently have two locations so you can enjoy riding some waves in either Kata or Patong Beach.

Go Scuba diving in Phuket

As in most of the Thai Islands, diving and exploring the underwater world is a must! Surround yourself with loads of tropical fish and coral as you visit some of Thailand’s best dive spots. Visit sites like Hin Daeng and Hin Muang dive sites where you can see the fantastic marine life on a steep wall and vibrant violet corals.
